Who is Jesus?

Jesus is God from the beginning, He created the everything. Then He came to earth to become human like us. He paid all of our sins in full payment and much much more. So that we can be with our Father and have an everlasting life.

God is a Trinity, 1 in 3 being

  1. God the Father
  2. God the Son
  3. God the Spirit

  • Multiply 5 bread and 2 fish to feed about 10,000 people
  • Turn water into wine
  • and much much more
